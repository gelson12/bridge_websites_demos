Where the River Meets the Pint

Nestled on the bustling banks of the Thames, The Waterside has served the heart of London since time immemorial. We honor the traditions of the British pub with cherished rituals: the slow pour, the quiet laughter over buttered toast, the warmth that gathers through generations.

Our interior? Oak beams, copper taps, and faded portraits of those who came before us. The beer is poured with care, the kitchen prepares food with grandmother’s recipes, and the staff – many of whom grew up here – know your name before you say it.

At The Waterside, we aren't just serving drinks. We're curating moments.
