Our Story

Hippo Tours began with a simple vision: to connect people with the wild beauty of our region through authentic, human-led adventures. We believe in small groups, deep local knowledge, and a profound respect for the land and its creatures.

Our guides are lifelong residents — ecologists, artists, and storytellers who know every secret path and singing bird. We don’t offer checklist tourism. We offer moments: the quiet rustle of moss underfoot, the sudden splash of fish in a hidden pool, the shared silence as a deer steps through the mist — and the story you’ll tell for years.
