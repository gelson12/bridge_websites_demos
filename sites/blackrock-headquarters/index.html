Our Story

A Legacy Built on
Conviction & Character

Founded in 1987 by Alexander Thorne, our firm was built on a singular conviction — that disciplined, patient capital consistently outperforms reactive speculation. What began as a single-family office on the eastern seaboard has grown into a multi-billion-dollar asset management institution serving endowments, foundations, pension funds, and sovereign wealth clients across four continents.

Our philosophy is rooted in the enduring principles of value orientation, counter-cyclical positioning, and rigorous fundamental research. We do not chase trends. We build portfolios that withstand the test of time — through bull markets, corrections, and every cycle in between. Every allocation we make is measured against a horizon measured not in quarters, but in generations.

Today, over 120 institutional partners entrust us with their capital — a responsibility we treat as our highest calling. Our team of 86 investment professionals spans offices in New York, London, Singapore, and Dubai, bringing local insight and global perspective to every decision.

Capital Endures. So does our commitment.
