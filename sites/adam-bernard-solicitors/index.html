About the Practice

A Tradition of Trust

Adam Bernard Solicitors has been a cornerstone of legal advice in Upton Park for as long as the community has relied on us. Based at 25 Barking Road, London E6 1PW, we serve a diverse clientele spanning generations of East London families.

Our team brings decades of combined experience across property law, family matters, wills and probate, and civil litigation. We believe that good law is rooted in honest conversation — taking the time to listen, explain, and guide you toward the best outcome for your situation.

Whether you are buying your first home, navigating a difficult family transition, or securing your family's future with a will, we stand beside you every step of the way.
