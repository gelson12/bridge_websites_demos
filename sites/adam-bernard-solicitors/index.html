Our Story

Twenty-eight years of London excellence

Founded by Adam Bernard in 1997, Bernard & Co. has grown from a single-desk chambers in Lincoln's Inn to one of the City's most respected boutique practices. We never lost the ethos that started it: that every client deserves a partner who knows their name, their story, and what's at stake.

Our team draws from barristers' chambers, Magic Circle firms, and the voluntary sector — a blend that gives us both firepower and heart. We are small enough to care, large enough to win.

Adam Bernard, Founder & Managing Partner
