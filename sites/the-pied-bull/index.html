Authentic Atmosphere

Warm, rustic interiors with timber beams and antique mirrors. The kind of place where time slows down and conversation flows as freely as our ale.

Local & Sustainable

Founded on Friendship, Sustained by Spirit

For as long as anyone can remember, The Pied Bull has stood as a pillar of community in Enfield EN2 9HE. What began as a quiet corner for neighbors to share stories over pints has grown into a cherished local institution.

Our walls speak softly of decades past — the chalk marks on the bar, the worn footrests, the faded photographs of matches won and seasons remembered. There’s no sign outside that says “Since 1883,” because the truth is, we don’t know the year. We only know that the ale is always good and the seats are always warm.

It’s not a pub you visit — it’s a place you return to.
