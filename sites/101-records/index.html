Sounds Captured, Not Manufactured

101 Records was born from a single turntable, a cracked amp, and a passion for music that refuses to be commercialized. Since our first cassettes were hand-labeled in my bedroom above our shared Croydon home, we've stayed true — no investors, no corporate logos, just pure sound.

Our studio on Keeley Road has become a sanctuary for artists who believe music is an act of rebellion. We press what moves us, when it moves us — no schedules, no targets, only integrity.

Our home is your home. Come in, have a cup of tea, and let the grooves speak.
