Our Legacy

Golden Cheng Hoo Boat began as a single paddle boat offering meals to fishermen along a forgotten river bend. Generations later, the boat is still here — no longer floating, but grounded in spirit. We hand-select every ingredient, follow the lunar calendar for harvests, and stir our pots with wooden spoons carved from the same tree that once anchored our first vessel. This is not merely dining. It is a ritual carried forward — where taste remembers time.
