Our Legacy

For over a century, the Cha Yong Ho Clan Assn S'pore has stood as a pillar of cultural continuity, preserving the unique heritage of our ancestors through community, ritual, and shared memory. While records were nearly lost in times of upheaval, the wisdom of our elders safeguarded the essence of our identity — the values, the stories, the reverence for kinship that defines us.

Today, we remain a quiet force — not seeking attention, but ensuring that future generations know from whence they came. Our halls are not museums; they are living spaces where traditions breathe, where cousins meet as family, and where the echoes of generation upon generation still resonate.

This is more than association — it is belonging.
