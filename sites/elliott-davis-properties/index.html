Our Story

A Legacy of Distinction

"London is not merely a city — it is the world's stage for uncompromising living."

Founded in 2006, Elliott Davies Properties has built a reputation as one of London's most discerning estate agencies. We operate at the intersection of heritage and modernity, representing properties that define the city's architectural soul — from Belgravia's Regency squares to the glass towers of the City.
