More Than a Bar

Alley Bar began as a whispered secret among those who knew the back entrance. No sign. No website. Just a flickering bulb above a wooden door. The walls still bear names scratched by regulars. The bar top is worn by 27 years of elbows. We don't serve drinks. We serve moments.

The owner started here in '99 with nothing but a fifth of bourbon and a broken stool. That stool? Still says I'm Scheduled. The last person to drink from it? There's a smear of lipstick and two salt grains - left by someone who never returned. Every bottle, every stain, every dent still remembers.
