Our Offerings

Brewed Traditions

Time-Honored Fare

Simple, honest dishes prepared with 100% British ingredients — from 28-day aged steaks and Sunday roasts to hand-battered cod and house-baked sourdough.

Timeless Culture

For over 177 years, our oak beams have held stories. Our fireplaces glow with the light of shared laughter. Our walls remember the names of those who've called this home.

A Place of Belonging

The Angelic began as a modest tavern in North London, where a single coal fire warmed the hearts of coal miners returning from shifts. Today, children of those miners sit beside new neighbors, and the same barkeep who poured the first pint now serves their grandchildren.

Every beam is reclaimed, every tile original, every stool worn smooth by countless generations of hands. We don’t chase trends—we preserve continuity. This is where time slows. Where strangers become regulars. Where one phrase still echoes: “It’s just like old times.”

History is not on the wall—it’s in the wood. Welcome home.
