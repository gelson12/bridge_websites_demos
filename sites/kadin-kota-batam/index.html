Our Mission

Kadin Kota Batam is dedicated to strengthening the local business ecosystem through collaboration, advocacy, and leadership. We serve as a bridge between enterprise and governance, ensuring that local entrepreneurs have the support and platform to thrive.

Our chamber brings together businesses of all sizes — from family-run shops to emerging startups — fostering an inclusive environment where every voice matters and every contribution helps shape the future of Batam's economy.
