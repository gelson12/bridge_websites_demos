Prime Cuts

Whole Animal Butchery

We honor the entire animal by crafting every part with precision. From nose to tail, our butchers transform each cut with tradition and reverence.

Local Delivery

Our cuts arrive frozen at peak freshness, packed in recycled insulation with dry ice. Hand-delivered by our team to your door within 24 hours.

Rooted in Tradition

For generations, our family has stood behind this counter, choosing only the finest animals raised with respect and slaughtered with reverence. We do not follow trends. We follow time-honored techniques passed down from master butchers who believed meat tells a story—and we are the keepers of this one.

Every cut carries the memory of place, animal, and hand. We work slowly, deliberately. We use no machines for Primary butchery. No plastic wraps. No preservatives. Just salt, time, and skill.
