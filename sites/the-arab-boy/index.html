Where Heritage Meets Hearth

Founded in the heart of London's SW15, The Arab Boy began as a dream of shared tables and hearty hospitality. We blend the warmth of the Eastern coffee house with the soul of Britain’s oldest ale houses, bringing together people of all cultures under one ceramic-tiled roof.

No robot scrubbed floors here — every scratch and scent is earned. Dust on the shelf? That’s spice history. Seven years of an unseasoned stool? It’s got a story. Your chair? It’s been waiting for you. We don’t serve food. We serve memory.
