Our Services

Signature Manicure

Our signature service combines relaxation with precision — a 90-minute treatment featuring gentle cuticle care, hand massage, and a flawless polish finish in your chosen hue from our curated palette.

Polish Artistry

From minimalist neutrals to intricate hand-painted designs, our nail artists bring your vision to life using premium, long-wear polishes with ultra-glossy protective coats.

Spa Pedicure

Unwind with our bespoke pedicure experience — warm aromatherapy soak, exfoliation, nourishing mask, and a soothing leg massage before your toes are adorned in serene, long-lasting color.

Our Sanctuary

Charming Nails was founded on the belief that beauty should be gentle, intentional, and deeply personal. Each visit is designed as a ritual — a quiet pause in your day to be pampered, seen, and restored.

Our studio is a haven of calm. Lavender-scented air, the soft glow of warm lighting, and meticulously curated music create a space where stress dissolves. Every detail — from the hand-woven linens to the vegan, cruelty-free products — reflects our commitment to elegance and ethics.

Our artists are not just technicians — they are storytellers who listen, observe, and translate your vision into wearable art. Whether it's a classic French tip or a custom design glowing with iridescent hues, your nails become an expression of your inner calm.
