Our Story & Passion

Bridge Test Cafe was founded in 2020 with a simple vision: to create a cozy haven where the community could gather over exceptional coffee and delectable treats. Nestled in the vibrant Rua de Teste, we quickly became known for our warm ambiance and commitment to quality.

We believe in the power of good coffee to bring people together. Every cup brewed and every pastry baked reflects our passion for craftsmanship and our dedication to providing a memorable experience. Come, be a part of our story.
