Who We Are

The Camat Lubuk Baja Office is the official local government administration serving the Lubuk Baja community. Our dedicated team works every day to ensure public services are delivered with integrity, efficiency, and deep respect for the people we serve.

Rooted in the traditions and needs of Lubuk Baja, we strive to be accessible, responsive, and transparent — building a governance model that reflects the values and aspirations of our region.

We believe in community-led development and work in close partnership with local organizations, residents, and stakeholders to create lasting, positive change.
