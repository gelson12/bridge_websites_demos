How We Got Here

It was supposed to be a coffee shop. We needed three shovels and a glut of painted pallets. What followed was a chaotic montage of discount lumber, misplaced decor, and a team with too much ambition and zero permits. Now we serve meals that defy physics, biology, and common sense.

Our servers have all been fired from five other restaurants. Our chef learned geometry from a broken chart. We are not open on your day off. We apologize in advance.
