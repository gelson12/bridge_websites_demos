More Than a Supermarket

Sheng Siong Supermarket at 266 Compassvale Bow is the heart of neighborhood life. We've built decades of trust by prioritizing freshness, affordability, and warmth over everything else. Our staff know our regulars by name, our shelves are stocked daily, and our commitment to quality is non-negotiable.

We are not a corporate chain — we are your neighbors, committed to making your daily shopping experience simple, clean, and kind. Just a short walk from Compassvale LRT, we serve families with the same care we'd show our own — with a smile, a nod, and the perfect durian always left on the counter just for you.
