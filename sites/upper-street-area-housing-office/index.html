About Upper Street Area Housing Office

As a trusted public service, Upper Street Area Housing Office has been committed to ensuring fair, safe, and dignified housing for every resident in the N1 1RE community. We operate with a steady hand and unwavering integrity, always putting people first.

Our team works every day to uphold transparent systems, community collaboration, and responsive service – because sturdy housing is more than shelter; it’s the foundation for thriving lives.
