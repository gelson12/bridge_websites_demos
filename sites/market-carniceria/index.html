Since the dawn of harvest, we've served neighbors

Market Carniceria honors the quiet rhythm of the land — where seasons dictate our offerings and every stem carries a story. We don't mark things up. We don't mass-produce. We source from those who know the soil and the sun, and we bring it to yours with reverence.

Our shelves are not for sale. They're for sharing — the crisp snap of celery, the perfume of ripe figs, the earthy warmth of fresh thyme. Taste what matters.
