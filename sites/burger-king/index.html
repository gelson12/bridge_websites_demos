Welcome to Burger King

For over 60 years, we've been flame-grilling burgers that stand out from the crowd. No microwaves. No pre-cooked patties. Just real, juicy beef cooked on open flames — the way you remember.

Our signature Whopper was invented with one goal: to be the biggest, boldest, most flavorful sandwich available. And we’ve never stopped pushing the boundaries — because when you have it your way, you deserve the best.

We believe in bold flavor, real ingredients, and serving up happiness in every bite. That's why we’re still here — serving up the original flame-grilled goodness.
