Our Story

Dollar Tree is your trusted neighborhood store where value meets variety. For generations, families have come to us for practical, everyday items at a price anyone can afford. Our shelves are always full, our staff are always friendly, and our prices never change — because everyone deserves to stretch their dollar.

Since opening our doors, our mission has stayed simple: offer something for everyone, every day, at a price that makes sense. No coupons. No membership fees. Only the best bargains you can find.
