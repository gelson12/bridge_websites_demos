A Place for People

More than a pub, The Union Bar is a gathering place where regulars aren’t just customers, they’re friends. The walls bear the scratches of decades and the clink of glasses echoes through the wood. No pretense, no frills — just good drinks, good food, and the kind of company you won’t find anywhere else in town.

Our staff are here because they care. They remember what you like. They know your name. We don't chase trends. We keep the bar warm.
