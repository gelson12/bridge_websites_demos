Casuarina Tree

Nestled on London Road in Mitcham, we are more than a restaurant — we are a sanctuary for mindful dining. Inspired by the quiet grace of the Casuarina tree, we design every moment of your visit to harmonize with nature’s rhythm — from the hand-thrown ceramics on your table to the scent of thyme drawn from our rooftop herb garden.

Our kitchen does not follow trends — it follows seasons. We serve not because we can, but because the earth demands it. The clay walls, woven light screens, and reclaimed timbers all echo the same gentle principle: take only what you need, give back what you can.

Our location is not a postcode — it is our soul. 407 London Road, Mitcham CR4 4BG, where the past meets the pause.
