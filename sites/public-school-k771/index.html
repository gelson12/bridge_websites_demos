Welcome to Public School K771

We are a vibrant, community-centered public school serving the families of Brooklyn. Our mission is to nurture every child’s potential with innovation, equity, and joy.

Our dedicated educators work together to create meaningful learning experiences rooted in collaboration, critical thinking, and compassion. We believe every child belongs here, and their unique voice matters.
