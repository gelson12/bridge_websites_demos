More Than a Pub, A Gathering

No neon signs here, no curated playlists. Just the clink of glassware, the crackle of an old fireplace, and the kind of conversation that’s been happening here since before anyone can remember.

Started informally as a leaning post for farmers and lorry drivers, Magnetic evolved without planning. The only rule: be kind, stay a while.

Our bar is worn smooth by decades of elbows, our floors still show the marks of well-loved boots. We don’t restore, we honor.
