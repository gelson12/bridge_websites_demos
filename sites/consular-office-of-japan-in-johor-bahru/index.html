Our Services

📜

Passport Services

Issuing and renewing Japanese passports for residents in Johor Bahru and surrounding areas, ensuring compliance with Japanese diplomatic protocols.

🪪

Visa Applications

Processing visa applications for travel to Japan, with detailed guidance on requirements and documentation for a smooth diplomatic experience.

🕊️

Civil Registration

Registration of births, marriages, deaths, and other civil matters for Japanese nationals abroad, preserving official records with precision.

Our Mission

As the official representation of Japan in Johor Bahru, we uphold the principles of mutual respect, cultural exchange, and diplomatic excellence. We serve Japanese nationals, local residents, and businesses with unwavering commitment to protocol and confidentiality.

Our office embodies the dignity and precision of Japanese foreign service, providing essential consular services with attention to detail and deep cultural understanding. We are dedicated to fostering strong bilateral ties between Japan and Malaysia through responsible, professional service.
