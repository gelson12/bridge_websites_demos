Our Story

Your neighborhood nail studio, elevated

Nail One was born from a simple idea: you shouldn't have to choose between convenience and quality. We bring salon-grade artistry, premium products, and a warm, welcoming vibe to your local block. Every set is a conversation — between color and shape, precision and creativity, our artists and you.

Using only the finest polishes, sterile tools, and techniques perfected over years of practice, we treat every nail like a canvas and every client like family. Step in for a quick touch-up or stay for the full spa experience — either way, you leave feeling like the best version of you.

— Mia Chen, Founder & Lead Artist
