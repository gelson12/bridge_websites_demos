Our story

Founded in 1971 by the Costa family in Barrow-in-Furness, we set out with one simple belief: that great coffee has the power to bring people together. Over half a century later, that belief still fuels every cup we pour.

We roast our signature Mocha Italia blend — a carefully guarded family recipe combining beans from six different origins — and serve it in cafes designed with the warmth of Art Deco elegance. Gold trim, sage accents, burgundy velvet. A space that feels like it has always been there.

Every barista is trained in the art of the perfect pour. Every guest is treated like family. Because life needs Costa.

— The Costa Family Barrow-in-Furness, since 1971
