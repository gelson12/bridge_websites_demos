Our Story

London's neighbourhood experts

Founded in 2012 on Stoke Newington High Street, Next Location was built on a simple belief — that finding a home should be as inspiring as the home itself. We combine deep local knowledge with a global outlook, serving buyers, sellers, landlords and tenants across London's most dynamic postcodes.

Every transaction we handle is personal. We invest the time to understand what matters to you, then draw on twelve years of market relationships to make it happen. No templates. No pressure. Just honest, expert guidance from people who love what they do.
