About Us

Hikmah Employment Agency PTE LTD is rooted in the heart of Joo Chiat Road, serving a diverse community with integrity and care. We believe employment is not just a transaction—it’s a partnership that transforms lives.

Our approach is guided by cultural awareness, deep listening, and personalized support. We treat every candidate and employer as an individual, recognizing that the right opportunity is more than a paycheck—it’s aligned purpose.

From entry-level placements to specialized roles, we’re committed to building meaningful connections that endure.
