The Grain That Became Glory

What began as a humble kitchen experiment with sticky rice and raw cane sugar grew into an obsession. In our workshop, time is the secret ingredient — we let each sweet evolve slowly, watching its transformation from humble grain to golden masterpiece.

Every bite carries the quiet warmth of dawn light on harvested fields, the hush of sugar crystals dissolving on a sun-warmed tongue, the memory of an old family recipe whispered across generations in soft, sticky whispers.

We don't just make sweets. We make moments.
