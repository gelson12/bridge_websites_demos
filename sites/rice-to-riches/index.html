From Soil to Sweetness

Founded in the heart of the countryside, Rice To Riches began with a simple conviction: that true luxury lies not in excess, but in authenticity. Every cocoa bean is sourced from smallholder farms we've partnered with for over a decade—where biodiversity thrives, soil health matters, and the rhythm of harvest shapes our recipe.

Our kitchen is owned by a family that has refined confectionery through five generations. We do not scale. We preserve. We listen to the earth. The subtle variations in flavor from one season to the next? That’s our signature. Our products carry the memory of rain, sun, and soil—not color additives or emulsifiers.

Each candy is wrapped in handmade paper embedded with wildflower seeds, allowing you to plant your pleasure after savoring it.
