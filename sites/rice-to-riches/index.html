More Than Confections

At Rice To Riches, our confections are more than sweet treats — they're stories captured in sugar, spice, and toasted grain. We believe the transformation of humble rice into golden, fragrant riches is a metaphor for life: patience, refinement, and artistry. Each batch is made in small quantities by hands that remember the touch of their ancestors.

We source our ingredients from family farms equivalent to sacred ground. Our recipes remain unchanged by time — never modernized by haste. Here, indulgence is ritual, and every bite resonates with memory.
