Time-Honored Craft, Modern Relevance

For over three decades, Squires Design has stood as a pillar of quality and authenticity in the signmaking industry. At 1 New Lane Hill, our artisans blend generations-old techniques with thoughtful innovation, creating signs that don't just communicate—they endure.

Every letter is hand-set. Every metal is hand-finished. Every color is matched with the precision of master painters. We believe a sign is not merely an advertisement—it's an heirloom, a commitment to permanence in an ephemeral world.

Our clients are communities, institutions, and businesses that understand quality. They choose us because they know that when they place their trust in our hands, they're investing in something that won't fade, corrode, or be forgotten.
