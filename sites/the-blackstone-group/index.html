Our Heritage

Built at 345 Park Avenue

Founded in 1985 by Peter G. Peterson and Stephen A. Schwarzman, The Blackstone Group has grown from a single Manhattan office into a global alternative asset management institution with over $1 trillion in assets under management.

Our name derives from a fusion of the founders' surnames — "Schwarz" (German for black) and "Peterson" (Greek for stone). A monolith of discipline, partnership, and long-term value creation.
