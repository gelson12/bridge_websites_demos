Rooted in Cluny

Since opening quietly in 2020 on a quiet street in Singapore, Roia has cultivated spaces where the rhythm of nature meets the ritual of the table. The menu changes weekly, guided by what the earth provides — never fixed, always reverent.

Our chefs move through each day with quiet intention: tending soil, tasting, fermenting, and preserving. There are no reservations for the sake of it. This is not a place to be seen. It is a place to arrive.
