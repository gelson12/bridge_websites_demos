Our Legacy

Founded in Hoboken, New Jersey, John Wiley & Sons has been at the forefront of scholarly publishing for over two centuries. Our mission is to connect knowledge and insight with those who seek it — reliably, rigorously, and responsibly.

We collaborate with the world’s most respected researchers, institutions, and educators to bring authoritative content to libraries, classrooms, and research labs across the globe. Our commitment to quality, ethical publishing, and open access continues to shape the future of academic communication.
