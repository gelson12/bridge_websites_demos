What We Pour

Proper Pints, Proper Pub

No frills. No pretence. Just great beer, proper food, and every game live. This is your local.

36 Pumps & Counting

Four cask ales — London Pride, Doom Bar, Landlord, and a rotating guest — alongside 20+ keg lines, 8 craft taps, and a fridge full of the world's best bottles. If it's not here, it's not worth drinking.

Every Game. Live.

Three SKY boxes, two BT Sport screens, Premier Sports, and our 12-foot cinema projector. From the 12:30 kick-off to the 8pm Champions League, your seat is waiting. Reserve a table for every Arsenal home game.

Scran That Hits

Massive homemade pies, the legendary Flag Burger (8oz dry-aged beef, smoked bacon, Stilton), loaded fries, and Sunday roasts that sell out before noon. Vegan options. Kids welcome til 8pm.

The Back Room

Pool table, darts board, two quiz machines, and our weekly Thursday pub quiz (7:30pm, 2 quid entry, cash prize). Or just grab a corner, sink into a worn leather chair, and watch the world go by.

Our Story

Three Decades at the Corner

The Corner Flag has been the beating heart of Hornsey Road since 1987. What started as a small Irish boozer with one TV and a dodgy bacon sandwich has grown into North London's most beloved football pub — without losing a scrap of its soul.

Same dark wood bar. Same tiled floor worn smooth by decades of boots. Same barmaid Karen who's been pulling pints since '92 and knows everyone's order before they reach the bar. The framed shirts on the wall — one from Highbury, one from White Hart Lane — sit side by side because rivalries stop at the door.

This is not a gastropub. Not a "sports bar." It's a proper local — the kind they don't make anymore. And we're bloody proud of it.
